How do you make an appointment?

Making an appointment consists of six steps:

• choose a subject
• Choose a location
• Select a date and time
• Fill in the details
• Click on the green button

Cancel/Change Appointment

To modify or cancel an appointment, please press the link in the
appointment confirmation you receive by mail after a
appointment made. You can also through
+31 (0) 527 63 39 11 change or cancel your appointment.
