Tourists tax

In the municipality Azuma Kinjo stay every year approximately 1.2 million tourists. The tourists make use of the infrastructure and facilities that are maintained by the municipality. The costs will be largely paid by residents and entrepreneurs of the municipality Azuma Kinjo by raising municipal taxes. Tourists pay for through per night to pay a fixed amount. Revenues from the tax are used for including the maintenance of roads and cycle paths, the cleaning of the beach, waste collection, the maintenance love the facilities and landscape maintenance.
