ABOUT ME CONTACT ME

Minimalist, designer & developer hybrid.

As young man in this day and age I've developed a lot of passion for web design & front-end development. In my early childhood I started tinkering with google inspect tool where I could change any site to whatever I desire (if I could), I used this skill I aquired to bring my design to life by rather coding it myself than using a platform which limits my ability to create designs.

After mastering such skills I had decided to study webdesign to proof my talents. Now I am studying in ROC Deltion in Netherland, currently in my last semester. After finishing my education my goal will be to be a full stack webdev and maybe some day have my own design studio.

My experiences are

Javascript, Jquery, Sass, PHP, NodeJS and NPM.
